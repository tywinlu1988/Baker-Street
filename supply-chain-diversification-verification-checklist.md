## Section D: Red Flag Triggers (escalate to CEO immediately)

- [ ] Samsung 2nm yields below 60% through Q1 2027
- [ ] Intel Foundry announces restructuring, spin-off, or major customer loss
- [ ] US intelligence assessment changes to "credible threat within 12 months"
- [ ] TSMC Arizona 3nm slips beyond H2 2027
- [ ] TSMC declares force majeure on any customer
- [ ] Foundry porting NRE exceeds budget by >25%
- [ ] Any secondary foundry first silicon fails >80% of performance targets

---

## Decision Rules

**If 2+ red flags in Section A:** Convene emergency board. Consider accelerating dual-source timeline, increasing buffer inventory to 6 months, and engaging with US government (Silicon Pact $500B includes provisions for critical supply assurance).

**If 3+ items in Section B are >2 weeks past target:** Pause new feature development. Reallocate engineering resources to porting. This is now the company's #1 operational priority.

**If any red flag in Section D:** CEO notifies board within 24 hours. No exceptions.

---

## Verification Protocol

To verify each status claim:
1. Attach source URL or document reference
2. Have a second person (not the reporter) independently confirm
3. Store in company shared drive under `supply-chain/verification/`
4. Review at weekly all-hands — max 5 minutes
